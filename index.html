I wrote this tool to help me understand numbers in different units. Try:
  • Energy: 100 TWh, 123 ktoe, 1000 therms or 1 boe
  • Power: 100 MW, 10 mscm/d
  • Area: 100 ares 10.3 hectares or 19 m2
  • Emissions: 1 pgC or 700 MtCO2e
You can also see a list of all the units that this tool knows.

This tool was produced by Tom Counsell. It is open source. Make a copy and report problems at github. The underlying data is in units.tsv and comparisons.tsv (You can open those files in Excel). I have written some instructions on how to add or edit units.
